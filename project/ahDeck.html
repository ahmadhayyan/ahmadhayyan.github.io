ahDeck is an application or a virtual interface that can open many applications and files more easily and quickly. ahDeck can also make your desktop look cleaner because you no longer need to put all the applications and files you regularly use on your desktop anymore.

Website : ahdeckstore.com

• Super Fast - You can open the ahDeck interface from anywhere on your screen whenever you want.
• Easy To Use - Simply press Alt + Right Click and the ahDeck interface will appear around your cursor.
• Customizable - Customize the 12 buttons to open the apps and files you like and also change the button color at will.
• Lifetime Support - One-time purchase, so you can keep your ahDeck app updated.
