Fogponic Plant Grower Box is a plant growing tool that uses the fogponic growing method, where the fogger will convert the nutrient and water solutions into particles measuring 5 - 30 µm to be sprayed on plants, and can work automatically by relying on the Arduino Nano microcontroller. This tool has a 10W LED light that can be adjusted to the brightness of the red and blue colors to resemble the sun so that the tool is suitable when used indoors. Another capability of this tool is that it can monitor the humidity of the tool, the water temperature, and the water level limit for the fogger. The data will be displayed on the LCD and the tool will automatically adjust the situation to match the ideal conditions needed by the plant, for example the tool will turn on the fogger when the humidity of the tool is below the specified limit and turn off the fogger when the humidity of the tool is above the specified limit.

Features :
- Can be used indoors
- Monitor the humidity of the tool, the water temperature, and the water level limit
- Display the data on the LCD
- Automatically adjust the situation to match the ideal conditions needed by plant

Benefits :
- Solve the problem of shrinking agricultural land in Indonesia
- Reduce cases of chemical poisoning in food, so that it is useful to help people maintain health quality
